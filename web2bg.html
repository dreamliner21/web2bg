Introduction

Hello, I am Paul, a high school student in the city of Semarang. This time I want to discuss the problem of "THE YOUNG PEOPLE WHO ARE LAZY TO READ". Curious? let's go to reading!

Causes

We cannot deny technological advances in the information sector. This progress will cause young people to become lazy about reading because young people will be more inclined to play online games instead of reading. This attitude is what makes young people are no longer interested in reading, whether reading story books or novels.

Solutions

Because there is advanced technology in the information sector, the steps we can take are to create a story through online media and create literacy activities, both at school, in the scope of work, or in society. This activity will have an impact on people's lifestyles, those who were previously lazy about reading will now be more enthusiastic about reading.

Conclusion

The conclusion of the problem that has been discussed is the reduced interest in reading among young people nowadays, caused by advances in technology in the information sector. This progress is what makes young people increasingly lazy to read.
